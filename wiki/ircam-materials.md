Collecting sourcing information for [IRCams](http://publiclaboratory.org/notes/warren/10-17-2012/prototype-raspberry-pi-based-dual-ir-cam):

* 2x USB Webcam (SYBA, same as in the DIY Spectrometry Kit), one modified for near-infrared
* 1 Raspberry Pi
* 2 USB > Mini-USB cables
* 1 SD card
* 1 cardboard box
* 1 USB power supply, i'm ordering one that runs on AA batteries, and using a Micro-USB power supply for now

###Webcams

Requirements (for spectrometers too):

* square side at 90 deg
* min 720 HD
* USB
* easy to replace/remove filter
* small enough to fit in a 1" Type-LB conduit box
* <$10 each, min quantity 1000 or less
