##Public Lab Posters for the 1st Ever White House Maker Faire

These posters are being displayed at the 1st White House Maker Faire. They show a broad array of the work being done by the Public Lab community, from Grassroots Mapping to Do-it-Yourself spectrometry.  All the work is open source. Enjoy!
