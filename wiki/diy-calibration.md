by [sara](../profile/sara)

April 26, 2012 17:34 | Tags: 

----

This is a page for aggregating and discussing issues of calibrating DIY tools. It will be an archive that documents the various approaches to calibrating data and tools that DIY project have taken. So that projects sharing calibration issues can learn from approaches taken in different projects.
