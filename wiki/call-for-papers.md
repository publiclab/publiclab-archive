## How does it work

Call for papers is a page for researchers to know relevant conferences, journals, etc., to submit their work.

## Important info

* Conference name and URL

* Date

* Description

* Open Source? (Y/N)

* Local

* Deadline

## Comments section

The comments page is a place for people to discuss their publications, ask for reviews before submitting their work, finding a co-author, etc.
