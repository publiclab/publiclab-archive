# Archiving

by [gonzoearth](../profile/gonzoearth), [warren](../profile/warren)

March 09, 2011 00:13 | Tags: 

----

On our main map archive, our thought was to have a few directory levels, so URLs work out to:

http://drndl.grassrootsmapping.org/&lt;location&gt;/&lt;year-month-day-sitename&gt;/&lt;format&gt;/&lt;file&gt;.&lt;extension&gt;

resulting in:

http://archive.publiclaboratory.org/cambridge/2011-6-22-massachusetts-cambridge-mit-knightcivicmediaconference/

* redundant, but good to have everything in a named folder, and also good to have a descriptive filename

http://archive.publiclaboratory.org/cambridge/2011-6-22-massachusetts-cambridge-mit-knightcivicmediaconference/raw - raw images

http://archive.publiclaboratory.org/cambridge/2011-6-22-massachusetts-cambridge-mit-knightcivicmediaconference/tms/openlayers.html - openlayers

http://archive.publiclaboratory.org/cambridge/2011-6-22-massachusetts-cambridge-mit-knightcivicmediaconference/tms/2011-6-22-massachusetts-cambridge-mit-knightcivicmediaconference.html - google maps
