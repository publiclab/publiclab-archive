The Public Laboratory for Open Technology and Science (Public Lab) is open for anyone to join and will always be free.

##What does it mean to join Public Lab?##

Being a **community member** may include one or more of the following:

* [being registered on this website](/signup)
* participating in at least one [mailing list](/lists) to learn from, contribute to, and gain recognition within the community
* using Public Lab tools outside or offline
* Other! Read more ideas [on the contribute page](/contribute)

As of September 2014, you can also become a **sustaining member** -- someone who supports the Public Lab 501(c)3 not-for-profit mission of democratizing inexpensive and accessible Do-It-Yourself techniques to create a collaborative network of practitioners who actively re-imagine the human relationship with the environment.
**Read more: http://www.publiclab.org/donate**
