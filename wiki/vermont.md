*Lead photo: Early experiments in Vermont with the Public Lab's infrared camera tool.*

**Vermont is part of the [Northeast region](http://publiclab.org/wiki/northeast).**
<p></p>
<p></p>
Public Lab organizers <a href="http://publiclab.org/people/cfastie">Chris Fastie</a> and <a href="http://publiclab.org/people/nedhorning">Ned Horning</a> are involved in multispectral imaging hardware, multispectral image processing, control and monitoring of airborne cameras, agricultural and vegetation mapping and imaging, and spectroscopy. 
<p></p>.
