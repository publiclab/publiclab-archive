# North Shore, Mass, Mapping Meetup: Pavilion Beach, Ipswich -- April 6, 2013

by [wward1400](../profile/wward1400), [Hudonnoodles](../profile/Hudonnoodles)

February 01, 2013 12:45 | Tags: [balloon-mapping](../tag/balloon-mapping), [kite-mapping](../tag/kite-mapping), [boston](../tag/boston), [event](../tag/event)

----

Let's have a Boston PLOTS meet up on **Saturday, April 6, 2012** at Great Neck Park and Pavilion Beach in Ipswich, MA. We'll have more logistical information soon, but let us know if you're available on April 6 to meet up at Great Neck Park for a skill share/mapping session and potential potluck. Feel free to invite your friends -- all skill levels welcome.

To note: Pavilion Beach and Great Neck Park will provide a sample site for the North Shore area with a sandy shoreline, municipal/state/federal regulated land, private land, marine habitat, wetlands, and tidal flats in close proximity.  We want to bring local residents from the surrounding areas to a central location and expand on community maps around <a href="https://mapknitter.org/map/view/plum-island-map-3-13-13">Plum Island Sound</a>.

We plan to meet at the parking lot at 142 Little Neck RD, Ipswich, MA, and then explore closer along the shoreline for mapping purposes. 

<strong>Details</strong>
Date: Saturday, April 6, 2013
Location: <a href="http://northshorekid.com/directory/great-neck-park">Great Neck Park</a> and <a href="http://newburyportkayakreport.wordpress.com/2009/08/23/pavilion-beach-sandy-point/">Pavilion Beach</a>, Ipswich, MA
Time: 12PM until there's no more wind, we run out of helium or we get hungry
Contact: email "plots-boston@googlegroup.com"

###Interested in participating:
RSVP: http://bostonplotsspring2013.eventbrite.com/
Coordinate ridesharing at <a href="https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc?key=0Ap88kieYRag1dDQxUHh2bWZDYkRhbHpHTHh6TjlxZUE&usp=sharing"> this google spreadseet</a>
Coordinate shuttle from the <a href="http://www.mbta.com/schedules_and_maps/rail/lines/?route=NBRYROCK&direction=O&timing=S&RedisplayTime=Redisplay+Time">Ipswich Commuter Rail Station</a> at <a href="https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc?key=0Ap88kieYRag1dDQxUHh2bWZDYkRhbHpHTHh6TjlxZUE&usp=sharing"> this google spreadsheet.</a>
Or bike out to Pavilion Beach from the Rail Station.
