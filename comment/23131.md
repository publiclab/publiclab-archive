Answers we came up with at our workshop include: 

- don't dump any stormwater pollutants, ([see stormwater pollutants question](https://publiclab.org/questions/stevie/06-19-2018/what-are-sources-of-stormwater-pollution)) 
- making sure storm drains aren't blocked, 
- clean out visible debris
