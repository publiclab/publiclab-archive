What about, as [@tonyc](/profile/tonyc) explored, we [photo a GPS unit](https://publiclab.org/notes/tonyc/07-01-2016/capturing-location-information-with-trap-cam) when we start it up? What if we use a "proof of existence" app like [CameraV](https://exposingtheinvisible.org/resources/obtaining-evidence/harlo-holmes) to video the start and end of the timelapse run, pointing it at the same GPS/clock as the timelapse camera is, so the authenticated CameraV video stands as a "chain of custody" confirmation -- kind of like how a XX is used to sync audio and video tracks in filmmaking?
