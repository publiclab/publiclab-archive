Environmental Science is an interdisciplinary study consisting of many different sciences such as biology, chemistry, physics, etc. Environmental Science is the study of the environment or in other words the world itself and the affect biotic and abiotic factors have on the environment. Biotic factors such as humans, animals, and plants. Abiotic factors being things like the air, water, chemicals, etc.
