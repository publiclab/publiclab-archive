Environmental Science is an interdisciplinary field of study that involves biotic, abiotic, soil, water, air, health, and safety of the public as well as biology to stabilize the environment.  The study  environmental science plays a crucial role in the survivability of the people and the environment as our coexistence is contingent to each other. It is very important for us to nurture, to understand and be the shepherd of our environment as our responsibility to ensure present and future generations stability.
