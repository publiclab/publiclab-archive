# Public Lab and Cuidadano Inteligente collaboration

by [Shannon](../profile/Shannon) June 27, 2011 16:02

June 27, 2011 16:02 | Tags: [protest-mapping](../tag/protest-mapping)

----

Thanks to the Knight Foundation and voters at the Knight conference,PublicLab will be able to collaborate with <a href="http://www.votainteligente.cl">Cuidadano Inteligente</a> in Chile to stream live video of protests by protesters. More information on the collaboration will be posted soon.
