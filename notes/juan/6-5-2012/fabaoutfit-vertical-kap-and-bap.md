The <a href="http://www.faba.in">FABA Collective</a> (<strong>F</strong>oto <strong>A</strong>Ã©rea a <strong>B</strong>aja <strong>A</strong>ltura, from Spain) proposes another equipment for vertical photography at low altitude from kite (KAP) or balloon (BAP), at low cost, named FABAoutfit. It contains the following elements: 

1) FABArig (a recycling bin and 2 S-hooks from Ik*a, 4 rubber bands (take from <a href="http://publiclaboratory.org/wiki/pet-bottle-rubber-band-rig">PET Bottle & Rubber Band Rig</a>) and a classic <a href="http://www.kaper.us/basics/BASICS_picavet.html">picavet</a> using 9 meters of braided nylon, 4 cable ties and one ring)

2) FABAwinder (in fact, an electric fence reel and 450 meters of braided nylon 1.3 mm)

3) FABAhangup (from 2 spring bed)

Your reviews and comments are welcome.
