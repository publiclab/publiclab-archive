This week in the Public Lab community newsletter, get ready for events in Atlanta, New Orleans and Salisbury (Vermont) and participate in the upcoming OpenHour. Enjoy!

**Upcoming events**

OpenHour: Monday September 1st, 8:00pm EST. Join us for an “Open” OpenHour, come with your thoughts, ideas and questions. Meet us on the [OpenHour page](http://publiclab.org/wiki/openhour) for a forum discussion. See you on Monday!

On September 18th, join grassroots mappers in New Orleans to work on mapping techniques and creating mapping rigs for upcoming Fall projects: [http://publiclab.org/notes/stevie/08-26-2014/new-orleans-training-event ](http://publiclab.org/notes/stevie/08-26-2014/new-orleans-training-event )

Join Public Labbers in Vermont for the [annual LEAFFEST gathering](http://publiclab.org/notes/cfastie/08-26-2014/l-e-a-f-f-e-s-t) September 21 and 22. Camp, cook and work on open science projects!

Public Lab Organizer, Jack Summers will be attending the Atlanta MakerFaire in early October to discuss his work with hydrogen sulfide sensing. If you’re in the area, let Jack know you’ll be around! [http://publiclab.org/notes/JSummers/08-28-2014/atlanta-maker-faire](http://publiclab.org/notes/JSummers/08-28-2014/atlanta-maker-faire ) 

The Fall Public Lab Barnraising will be November 14-16 in Cocodrie, LA. [Sign up today](http://publiclab.org/wiki/barnraising)!
