This week in the Public Lab community newsletter, join the upcoming OpenHour, take advantage of the last week of back to school sales on kits and join events in NYC, Philadelphia and New Orleans over the next couple of weeks. Enjoy!

**Announcements**

Now through September 30th, take advantage of the [Back to School sale](http://publiclab.org/notes/Natalie/08-29-2014/back-to-school-sale-happening-now-through-september-30th) on all Public Lab kits! Enter discount code ###BACK2SCHOOL at checkout for a 20% discount.
