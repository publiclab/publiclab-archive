This week in the Public Lab community newsletter, join the Public Lab nonprofit team, check out updates on air quality monitoring projects and join the largest Barnraising yet in a couple of weeks. Enjoy!

**Announcements**

The Public Lab nonprofit is hiring! We’re looking for a Data Ambassador to join the team full time and an Administrative Coordinator to join us part time. More information on both jobs can be found here: [http://publiclab.org/wiki/public-lab-jobs](http://publiclab.org/wiki/public-lab-jobs ) 

Did you know that Friday was a Public Lab's anniversary? One year ago we received our 501(c)3 tax exempt status! Celebrate with us by becoming a Public Lab sustaining member today! [http://publiclab.org/donate](http://publiclab.org/donate)
