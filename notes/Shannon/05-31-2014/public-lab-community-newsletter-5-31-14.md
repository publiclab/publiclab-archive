# Public Lab community newsletter 5.31.14

This week in the Public Lab community newsletter, join us today, tomorrow and Monday for events in NYC and New Orleans, grab your camping gear and participate in the Spring Barnraising next week in Plymouth, MA and check out updates from Google Summer of Code participants. Enjoy!

**Announcements**

The new Infragram interface is now the default! [Infragram.org](Infragram.org) - with much-improved presets, easier workflow, and loads of tweaks and improvements.

The first Tool focused Spring Barnraising, a more tech-centric event to be held near Plymouth, MA, is next weekend, June 7-8. [Check out the details and join](http://publiclab.org/notes/Hudonnoodles/04-30-2014/northeast-tool-focused-barnraising-june-6-8-2014)! 

Save the Date for the Annual Fall Barnraising in Cocodrie, Louisiana. We’ll be convening this year from November 14-16.

**Upcoming events**

Today! Join Public Labbers in Brooklyn from 2-4pm est for the NYC World Science Fest spectrometry meet-up: [http://publiclab.org/notes/liz/05-23-2014/come-try-spectrometry-at-science-hack-day](http://publiclab.org/notes/liz/05-23-2014/come-try-spectrometry-at-science-hack-day)

Tomorrow, June 1st, Gena Wirth will be leading a kite mapping expedition to document volunteer wetland planting in Jamaica Bay (NYC). Email geneva.wirth@gmail.com to connect and check out information here: 
[http://publiclab.org/notes/liz/05-30-2014/june-1-jamaica-bay-wetland-planting-photography](http://publiclab.org/notes/liz/05-30-2014/june-1-jamaica-bay-wetland-planting-photography)

Monday, June 2nd in New Orleans there will be a Barataria mapping project meeting at 6pm cst. The meeting will be held at Propeller, 4035 Washington Ave. Please email Stevie@publiclab.org for more information.
