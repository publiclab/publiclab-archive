**Upcoming events**

If you’re attending the [Open Hardware Summit](http://2014.oshwa.org/) in Rome on September 30 and October 1, check out Public Lab Organizer, Hagit Keysar’s, talk on Open Hardware and Civil Architecture.

This OpenHour will be on “Events and Event Hosting” with guest presenters Jen Hudon, Katie Gradowski and Danielle Kraus! Learn more on the OpenHour page.

Join Public Labbers in New Orleans on October 11th for the kickoff to a year of urban restoration mapping: [http://publiclab.org/notes/stevie/09-11-2014/new-orleans-urban-restoration-mapping-blitz](http://publiclab.org/notes/stevie/09-11-2014/new-orleans-urban-restoration-mapping-blitz)

As part of [The Hacktory’s](http://old.thehacktory.org/) fall workshop series, Sean McGinnis will be [leading a workshop](http://publiclab.org/notes/SeanKMcGinnis/09-18-2014/balloon-mapping-raspberry-pi-workshop-the-hacktory) (in Philadelphia) on October 18th on the basics of balloon, kite and pole mapping and using Raspberry Pi as a sensor base while mapping.
