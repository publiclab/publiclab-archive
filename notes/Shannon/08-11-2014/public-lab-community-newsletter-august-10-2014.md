# Public Lab Community Newsletter August 10, 2014

by [Shannon](../profile/Shannon) August 11, 2014 02:54

August 11, 2014 02:54 | Tags: [newsletter](../tag/newsletter), [newsletter-archive](../tag/newsletter-archive)

This week in the Public Lab community newsletter, provide input on MapKnitter annotations, the SkyCam and curriculum for the Infragram and start thinking about packing your bags to join Public Labbers during the November Barnraising in Louisiana. Enjoy!

**Announcements**

The theme for the November 13-16  Barnraising is, From the Field to Stories of Change. Signup for the gathering [here](http://publiclab.org/wiki/barnraising).

There will be no OpenHour on Monday August 11th. Stay tuned for a new [OpenHour](http://publiclab.org/wiki/openhour) schedule coming soon!

**New and ongoing projects**

With generous support from Raspberry Pi Foundation, Public Labbers have been creating curriculum for the Infragram. Check out their progress here: [http://publiclab.org/notes/amandaf/08-09-2014/curriculum-for-infragram](http://publiclab.org/notes/amandaf/08-09-2014/curriculum-for-infragram)

As Google Summer of Code 2014 winds down, take a look at updates and requests for input from two of the students who are working on [MapKnitter annotations](http://publiclab.org/notes/justinmanley/08-06-2014/mapknitter-annotations-interface-refactoring%20) and [SkyCam enhancement](http://publiclab.org/notes/mercyorangi/08-10-2014/sky-cam-enhancement-user-guide-app-icon-added).

Jack Summers posted a note on the soon to launch WheeStat kit: [http://publiclab.org/notes/JSummers/08-07-2014/wheestat-kit-assembly](http://publiclab.org/notes/JSummers/08-07-2014/wheestat-kit-assembly)
