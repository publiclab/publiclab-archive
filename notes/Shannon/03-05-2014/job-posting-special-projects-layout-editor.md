# Job Posting: Special Projects Layout Editor

by [Shannon](../../../profile/Shannon) | March 05, 2014 18:12

March 05, 2014 18:12 | Tags: [jobs](../tag/jobs)

----

**Public Laboratory for Open Technology and Science: Layout Editor**

**Start Date:** Late March 2014

**Location:** Remote

**Terms:** Contract

The Public Laboratory for Open Technology and Science (Public Lab) is a community--supported by a 501(c)(3) non-profit--which develops and applies open-source tools to environmental exploration and investigation. By democratizing inexpensive and accessible Do-It-Yourself techniques, Public Lab creates a collaborative network of practitioners who actively re-imagine the human relationship with the environment.

We are looking for someone to take over the Grassroots Mapping Forum editor and layout roles, as well as a few small design projects throughout the year. Our ideal candidate is a Public Lab community member with writing and editing experience and graphics experience as well as a proficiency with Adobe InDesign. The contract role will have roughly $6,000/year in work.

**Responsibilities**

_Grassroots Mapping Forum_

* Planning article length with authors using the GMF template system
* Coordinating and encouraging writers to complete submissions on time
* Collecting image resources in print-quality sizes
* Processing images for print in Photoshop
* Laying articles out in InDesign
* Reviewing with community before production of final document

_Annual Report_

* Produced in the early Spring of each year, design and layout of 8.5” x 11” document and web prep.

_Other Publications_

* Primarily digital publications, may include items such as maps book, packaging and instructions,  sample handout and physical logbook for field sampling.

**Skills, Knowledge and Abilities Required**

* Excellent communication skills–in written, verbal and online formats.
* Ability to meet deadlines and follow project budgets 
* Technical requirements: Adobe Suite, InDesign required. Familiarity with CSS, javascript and HTML is a plus.
* Ability to collaborate on writing activities amongst a highly diverse community of people from many different fields and experience sets.
* Knowledge and appreciation of Public Lab (community & nonprofit), its mission, its programs and services, and the ability to communicate them effectively.

Please send a cover letter, resume and a link to your website with work examples to Shannon@publiclab.org by March 21, 2014. No phone calls please.
