# What do you do if your environmental concern is linked to your neighbor's paycheck?

by [gretchengehrke](../../../profile/gretchengehrke) | July 10, 2017 21:20

July 10, 2017 21:20 | Tags: [question:question](../tag/question:question), [question:advocacy](../tag/question:advocacy), [question:communication](../tag/question:communication)

----

A big question we started to grapple with at the Appalachian Barnraising was what to do to make progress on your environmental concern when other people in your community (especially family members or friends) depend on paychecks from that industry. Where do you start, and how do you move forward? Have you had experience with this situation? What did you do?
