**Join Public Lab this Halloween at the [Queensbridge Tech Lab!][1]**

The world is filled with many uncovered mysteries and legends! 

-What does saltwater sound like? 
-What does pond water look like under a microscope? 
-How can DIY tools help you learn and share about air quality?

Come get messy and creative at the Tech Lab during this free hands-on pop-up science & tech workshop that all ages can engage with. Bring your ideas, curiosities: spooky costumes encouraged!

Date: October 31st
Where: [Queensbridge Tech Lab][2] 
Time: 2 - 5 PM
