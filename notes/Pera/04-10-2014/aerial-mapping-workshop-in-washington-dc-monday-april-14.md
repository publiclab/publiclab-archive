# Aerial mapping workshop in Washington DC - Monday, April 14!

by [Pera](../profile/Pera) | April 10, 2014 15:01

April 10, 2014 15:01 | Tags: [dc-baltimore](../tag/dc-baltimore), [first-time-poster](../tag/first-time-poster)

----

Aerial mapping and imagery workshop for State of the Map Sprint Day  
Monday, April 14 9am - 4pm in Washington, DC

Join Public Lab on Monday, April 14, for an extra fun, hands-on, aerial-mapping workshop in Washington, DC. Explore how to fly kites with cameras, stitch aerial images into maps with MapKnitter and use your own high resolution aerial imagery for tracing into Open Street Map.  

This workshop is part of the Open Street Map conference's Sprint Day. It is free, but pre-registration is required.  [Please sign up here. ](http://www.eventbrite.com/e/sprint-day-state-of-the-map-us-tickets-10533697573)
