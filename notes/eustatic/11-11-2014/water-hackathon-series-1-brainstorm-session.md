**WHEN**
Tuesday, November 18, 2014
6:00 PM – 9:00 PM

**WHERE**
Propeller Incubator
4035 Washington Avenue
New Orleans, LA 70125

[Get Map](http://maps.google.com/maps?q=4035+Washington+Avenue+New+Orleans+LA+70125)

DETAILS
Document data and research gaps identified by the October 21st Monitoring Water Challenges panelists. Attendees will brainstorm “hackable” solutions, with a focus on addressing the needs of impacted communities with creative and accessible research strategies!

Presenters:<br>
Emily Danielson (Policy and Program Strategy Manager, Institute of Women & Ethnic Studies)<br>
Stevie Lewis (Public Laboratory for Open Science and Technology)<br>
Don Blair (Public Laboratory for Open Science and Technology)<br><br>

Agenda:<br>
I. Overview of Monitoring Water Challenges session, what lessons were learned<br>
II. Overview of quantitative and qualitative methodologies<br>
III. Introduction to Public Lab<br>
IV. Water quality activity and hands on learning through creation of DIY monitoring technology<br>
V. Closing Discussion and Q&A<br>
