Please join us on April 20, 2013, to mark 3 years of BP's ongoing disaster, memorialize all that has been lost, and join the call for justice for the Gulf. 

 On Saturday, April 20th at 1pm in the French Quarter, you will have the opportunity to participate in a simple visual demonstration of the ongoing disaster along the Gulf Coast, which will be made into a short video and distributed on the internet to raise awareness about the urgent need for restoration and to hold BP accountable for the damages incurred. 

Please wear black.  The first 100 people to arrive will receive a free t-shirt designed for the event, which you can wear during the filming.
