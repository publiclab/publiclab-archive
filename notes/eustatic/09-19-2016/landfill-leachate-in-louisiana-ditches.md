###Background

There have been a number of complaints about orange sludge in the ditches along Woodlands hwy north of Belle Chasse in Plaquemines Parish. 

Let's Try to collect some water samples, and learn how to use public lab tools to gather some evidence of pollution's impact on our local waters.
