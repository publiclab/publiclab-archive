# Progress on NRG compositing from MapKnitter

by [warren](../profile/warren) January 04, 2012 01:21

January 04, 2012 01:21 | Tags: [saugus](../tag/saugus), [landfill](../tag/landfill), [waste](../tag/waste)

Work is progressing on a web-based multispectral compositing feature for [MapKnitter.org](https://mapknitter.org).

* See [notes and progress on GitHub](https://github.com/jywarren/mapknitter/issues/80) (and contribute!)
* above, see a test export from the Basurama/Harvard GSD's [map of the Saugus MA landfill](https://mapknitter.org/map/edit/saugus-incinerator-landfill) (and [infrared version](https://mapknitter.org/map/edit/saugus-incinerator-ash-landfill-infrared)), destination of all Cambridge, MA's trash.
* see the [accompanying OpenLayers browseable version](https://mapknitter.org/warps/saugus-incinerator-composite/good/nrg-blackbg/openlayers.html)

Eventually this should be doable from a modal dialogue for any MapKnitter map... familiar with GDAL and have some time? Get in touch on the mailing list!
