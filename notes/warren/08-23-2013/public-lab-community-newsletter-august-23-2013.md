# Public Lab Community Newsletter - August 23, 2013

by [warren](/profile/warren) | August 23, 2013 15:27

August 23, 2013 15:27 | Tags: [newsletter](/tag/newsletter), [blog](/tag/blog), [newsletter-archive](/tag/newsletter-archive)

----

Happy Friday, Public Lab! Lots happening this week between updates on two Kickstarters, hundreds of new potential contributors to our infrared photography project, and various announcements and upcoming events. Stay cool, summer’s almost over!

Remember -- you can [subscribe to this newsletter](/subscribe/tag/newsletter) or follow [via RSS](/feed/tag/newsletter.rss).

###Announcements

We’re proud to announce that [Public Lab is a Semi-Finalist](/notes/Shannon/08-22-2013/public-lab-selected-as-a-semi-finalist-for-the-2013-buckminster-fuller-challenge) for the Buckminster Fuller Challenge!

[Public Lab’s store](http://store.publiclab.org/products/balloon-mapping-kit) is now carrying balloon mapping kits with [a new box design](http://publiclab.org/notes/warren/08-20-2013/new-boxes-for-balloon-mapping-kits) by [Nickolas Peter Chelyapov](http://nickolaspeter.com).

###Upcoming Events 

[Planning and registration continues](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!topic/publiclaboratory/QzllpmxHGsM) for the October [Public Lab Barnraising in Cocodrie, Louisiana](http://publiclab.org/wiki/barnraising-2013)

Sunday Aug 25th, 11am-4pm in Manomet Massachusetts: Infrared balloon mapping event at Tidmarsh Farms with the MIT Media Lab’s Responsive Environments group and Public Lab http://ow.ly/oal7q 

The [Open Hardware Summit 2013](http://2013.oshwa.org) is coming up on September 6 at MIT (a number of Public Lab folks will be there!), and there is an [open call for nominations](http://2013.oshwa.org/2013/08/13/nominations-for-oshwa-board-member-positions/) to the board of the Open Source Hardware Association.

At Mozilla’s Mozfest in October in London, Public Lab has proposed a session on “Open source science: not just for scientists”: http://publiclab.org/notes/warren/08-21-2013/public-lab-sessions-mozfest-oct-25-7-2013 

###New and ongoing projects with Public Lab tools 

[Infragram webcam tests continue](http://publiclab.org/notes/warren/08-20-2013/infragram-webcam-conversion-test-with-i-zone-cameras) as part of the [Infragram Kickstarter project](http://kickstarter.com/projects/publiclab/infragram-the-infrared-photography-project)

[Infragram DIY Filter Packs](http://store.publiclab.org/products/infragram-diy-filter-pack) have [begun shipping from Portland Oregon](http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/publiclab/infragram-the-infrared-photography-project/posts/575227) as part of the Infragram Kickstarter project.

[Public Lab Organizers](/wiki/organizers) have compiled a “Give or get help!” [list of troubleshooting resources](http://publiclab.org/wiki/infragram#Give+or+get+help) for the hundreds of new Infragram contributors we’ll see in coming weeks and months. 

[Smartphone spectrometer injection molding samples](/notes/warren/08-20-2013/smartphone-spectrometer-molded-samples-have-arrived) have returned from [ProtoEdge](http://protoedge.com) and final plastics decisions are being made as part of [the DIY Spectrometry Kit Kickstarter](http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/jywarren/public-lab-diy-spectrometry-kit/posts/552874).

Japanese Public Labbers [recently took their first balloon mapping photos](http://twitpic.com/d9cv6n):

###Research Notes Highlights

Californian Public Labbers recently did coastal observations with kite mapping setups [in Malibu Lagoon & Surfrider Beach, Malibu, CA](http://publiclab.org/notes/MicheleTobias/08-20-2013/malibu-lagoon-surfrider-beach-malibu-ca)

Chris Fastie and others have been [exploring techniques for post-processing white balance](/notes/cfastie/08-20-2013/white-balance-recovery) on multispectral plant photographs.

[Contributor bc512](/profile/bc512) from Australia discusses [infrared vineyard monitoring with quadcopters](/notes/bc512/08-18-2013/mapping-vineyards-with-quadcopter-using-converted-canon-powershot-a495)

###Public Lab in the media this week

[Liz Barry](/profile/liz), Public Lab, and partners were featured [in a short piece in the New Yorker](http://www.newyorker.com/talk/2013/08/26/130826ta_talk_frazier) this week on p.22, which covered the [urban agriculture pole photography](/tag/5BF) they are doing in NYC.
