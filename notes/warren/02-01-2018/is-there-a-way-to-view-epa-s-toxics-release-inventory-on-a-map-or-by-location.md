I wanted to view nearby reports in the TRI, and via the EPA's site, I found:

  
https://www.epa.gov/enviro/tri-search  

  
When i entered my zip code -- 02903 -- i see this really long URL:

  
https://iaspub.epa.gov/enviro/efsystemquery.tri?fac\_search=primary\_name&fac\_value=&fac\_search\_type=Beginning&postal\_code=02903&location\_address=&add\_search\_type=Beginning2&city\_name=&county\_name=&state\_code=&selecttribe=&triballand=&tribedistance=Beginning3&sic\_type=Equal+to&sic\_code\_to=&naics\_type=Equal+to&naics\_to=&industry\_type=&chem\_name=&chem\_search=Beginning4&cas\_num=&program\_search=TRI&page\_no=1&output\_sql\_switch=TRUE&report=1&database\_type=TRIS  

  
Are there easier ways to search and view TRI reports of pollution on a map?
