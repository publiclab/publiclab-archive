Hello all -- happy Friday; we've got a LOT of events in coming weeks, so be sure to look for things happening near you! Want to add things to the newsletter? Email [organizers@publiclab.org](mailto:organizers@publiclab.org)

Remember -- you can [subscribe to this newsletter](http://publiclab.org/subscribe/tag/newsletter) or follow [via RSS](http://publiclab.org/feed/tag/newsletter.rss).

###Announcements

Public Labs has joined the Gulf Monitoring Consortium.  “working together to investigate and publicize pollution in the Gulf region” -- read more at http://www.gulfmonitor.org/about/

A new map of regional Public Lab groups has been posted at http://publiclab.org/places/  -- which also links to the [start a chapter page](http://publiclab.org/wiki/start-a-chapter)

Skytruth, a fellow (and founding) member of the Gulf Monitoring Consortium, is [crowdfunding an attempt](http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/skytruth-the-bakken) to use thermal photography to map gas flares from North Dakota's Bakken Shale. Read more here: http://blog.skytruth.org/2013/08/skytruth-the-bakken.html

###Upcoming Events 

Check out the **LEAFFEST weekend gathering in Salisbury, Vermont** on September 7 & 8 -- http://publiclab.org/wiki/leaffest (This is a great opportunity to get involved in infrared photography research.)

Barnraising topic sessions brainstorming has begun as the 2013 Public Lab conference approaches: http://publiclab.org/wiki/barnraising-2013#Session+Ideas 

[Farm Hack](http://www.youngfarmers.org/practical/farm-hack/) has asked if Public Lab would also join them at the Maine Common Ground Country Faire that same weekend! http://www.mofga.org/theFair

[Mathew](/profile/mathew) and [Shannon](/profile/shannon) are doing a couple of workshops next week in London. If you know anyone interested in participating, please contact Shannon at [shannon@publiclab.org](mailto:shannon@publiclab.org).

**Sept. 6th 18:30-22:30:** Kite/Balloon Mapping First workshop](http://www.meetup.com/Citizens-without-Borders/events/135709822/): making kites @ the [Mildmay Community Centre](http://goo.gl/maps/INHIo), N16 8NA, East London

**Sept. 7th 11:00-16:00:** [Kite/Balloon Mapping Second workshop](http://www.meetup.com/Citizens-without-Borders/events/135710872/): fly, map, & interpret @ the [Mildmay Community Centre](http://goo.gl/maps/INHIo), N16 8NA, East London

**Sun 8th Sept 14:00-17:30:** [DIY Spectrometry - introduction to spectrometry workshop](http://www.meetup.com/Citizens-without-Borders/events/136550582/) @ UCL, Gower St. main entrance.

**Mon 9th Sept 18:30-22:30:** [Testing the PLOTS DIY Spectrometer workshop](http://www.meetup.com/Citizens-without-Borders/events/136551862/): 'advanced' applications @ UCL, Gower St. main entrance

Join your fellow Public Labbers at **World Maker Faire** the weekend of September 21-22! http://makerfaire.com/new-york-2013/ -- We will be making a main stage presentation as well as staffing two (2!) tables: our own, and one inside the Farm Hack tent to highlight the Infragram. This is a great opportunity to hang out with some visiting Public Lab folks from out of town. I (Liz Barry) personally guarantee it will be a fun time. There will be a lot of visitors to our table so it's a great chance to speak about your own work if desired and make connections! Please email [liz@publiclab.org](mailto:liz@publiclab.org) if you can attend.
