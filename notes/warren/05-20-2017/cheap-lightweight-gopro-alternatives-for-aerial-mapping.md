Just following up on [the question](/questions/cherylh/05-15-2017/how-can-real-time-camera-monitoring-be-set-up) posted by @cherylh from [Fair Tech Collective](https://www.fairtechcollective.org/) with something that may be relevant to real-time video counter-surveillance --

Related to various efforts to use [smaller kites](/tag/mini-kite-kit) and [balloons](/tag/mini-balloon-kit) to do aerial mapping with lighter cameras, the Mobius Action Cam that many Public Labbers use is great, at ~$70 and ~40 grams, but I'm starting to see GoPro look-alikes show up on Amazon for even less, and with waterproof enclosures and sometimes even Wifi connectivity. I collected a few here, but have folks tried any of these, or have other suggestions for cheap, <100 gram cameras to fly on balloons and kites?

Some I've found include:

- $60, 61g, 2M,5M,8M,12M,16M - [https://www.amazon.com/Campark-Waterproof-Wide-Angle-Rechargeable-Batteries/dp/B01MXXP4EC/](https://www.amazon.com/Campark-Waterproof-Wide-Angle-Rechargeable-Batteries/dp/B01MXXP4EC/)
- $53, 1200x1800, 60g - [https://www.amazon.com/GBB-Waterproof-Camcorder-Detection-Rechargeable/dp/B01G6GWSJW/](https://www.amazon.com/GBB-Waterproof-Camcorder-Detection-Rechargeable/dp/B01G6GWSJW/) + waterproof case
- $50 12MP, not sure weight - AKASO - [https://www.amazon.com/AKASO-Waterproof-Camcorder-Rechargeable-Mounting/dp/B06XHNQ44X/](https://www.amazon.com/AKASO-Waterproof-Camcorder-Rechargeable-Mounting/dp/B06XHNQ44X/)
- $150, 8MP, 72g, GOPro Session - [https://www.amazon.com/dp/B01C80O0ZU/](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B01C80O0ZU/)
- Via @bronwen, a bunch here, too: [https://beebom.com/gopro-alternatives-2016/](https://beebom.com/gopro-alternatives-2016/)

Any help appreciated!

---------

**Update: **we now [carry a version of these in the Public Lab store](https://store.publiclab.org/products/waterproof-mini-camera)! And there is a [guide here to modifying one of these](https://publiclab.org/n/13742) to take [infrared/multispectral photos](/wiki/infragram).

The images they produce are nice, but not incredibly high quality.
