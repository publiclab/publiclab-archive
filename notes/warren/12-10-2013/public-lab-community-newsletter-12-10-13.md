# Public Lab community newsletter 12.10.13

by [warren](/profile/warren) | December 10, 2013 15:07

December 10, 2013 15:07 | Tags: [newsletter](/tag/newsletter), [newsletter-archive](/tag/newsletter-archive)

----

_(Above, [DJUltis](/profile/DJUltis)' MQI environmental recorder, posted last week. Read below to learn more!)_

Apologies for the late newsletter, but it's a busy time of year! The next newsletter will also be the last one before the New Year.

###Announcements

Amazon is now [listing Balloon Mapping Kits](http://www.amazon.com/Balloon-Mapping-Kit/dp/B00FZX4C7Y/) via Edmund Scientifics; this is in addition to the [Foldable Mini-spectrometer](http://www.amazon.com/Public-Lab-FOLDUP2-Smartphone-Spectrometer/dp/B00DSA70TS/) which we have listed ourselves. Please write a review if you are feeling up to it!

###Events

This weekend, New Yorkers hosted a Newtown Creek mapping in Brooklyn -- read the event listing here:  http://publiclab.org/notes/nicholas/12-02-2013/public-mapping-mission-newtown-creek

**Upcoming:** planning is in progress for some January [flare spectrometry](http://publiclab.org/tag/flare) meetups in the New Orleans area -- be in touch on the [Gulf Coast mailing list](http://publiclab.org/wiki/mailing-lists) if you're interested, and keep your ears open for more announcements!

###New and ongoing projects

Chris Fastie has been posting a great series on multispectral or Infragram photography, ranging from [photos of root vegetables](http://publiclab.org/notes/cfastie/12-08-2013/root-crops) to some of [ferns, mosses, and lichens](http://publiclab.org/notes/cfastie/11-27-2013/cryptogams). 

###Hot topics on the mailing lists

[Controlling automated water quality samplers wit an Arduino](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!topic/publiclaboratory/w6wcjrFApJY)

[Working simultaneously as a group in a MapKnitter.org project](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!topic/grassrootsmapping/IZqNx-dvuMA)

The Gowanus Canal Conservancy is [looking for new team members to work on STEM education](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!topic/plots-nyc/l2ICHVla3dw)

###Research Note highlights

[DJUltis](/profile/DJUltis) posted an [introduction to his MQI environmental recorder](http://publiclab.org/notes/DJUltis/12-09-2013/mqi-environmental-recorder), in a great lasercut case -- including a built-in thermal flashlight!

[Nicholas Johnson](/profile/nicholas) posted [a thorough guide on configuring CHDK](http://publiclab.org/notes/nicholas/12-05-2013/chdk-scripting) for scripting your camera to take photos while balloon mapping. 

[Nathanathan](/profile/nathanathan) has been exploring [using glow in the dark paint to calibrate camera color sensors](http://publiclab.org/notes/nathanathan/12-04-2013/glow-in-the-dark-paint-photos).

If anything was missed, please make sure to post a research note or send a message to the list. Have a great week everyone!

-- Jeff
