# DIY-spectrometer

by [TakeshiMatsumoto](../../../profile/TakeshiMatsumoto) | March 20, 2018 22:26

March 20, 2018 22:26 | Tags: [spectrometer](../tag/spectrometer), [diy-bio](../tag/diy-bio), [hardware](../tag/hardware), [first-time-poster](../tag/first-time-poster)

_**Motivation**_ï¼š We are dealing with piezo crystal products.
We are considering new analysis technique that combining piezo sensor 
and spectrometer measurements. However, commercially available 
multichannel spectrometer is very expensive, $2,000-$4,000/p . So we 
decided to develop by our self.

![Measuring system](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/020/399/351/6dcc8c2ff4401e4b52112f5e591cb3a7_original.jpg?w=700&fit=max&v=1519865390&auto=format&q=92&s=aff92248646120c8a19ad3552392cb09)Measuring system  
_**System**_ : In the measurement system, connect the PC and 
spectrometer via USB, and measurement software perform data acquisition 
and graph display. 

Data can be saved as a text (csv) file.

In
addition, initial calibration is performed at the time of manufacturing
and shipping, it is possible for users to recalibrate easily.

Filters and slits can be changed arbitrarily by the user depending on the wavelength and intensity of light.

The spectrometer uses mirrors and diffraction gratings. The 
photograph shows that a spectrum with incidence of a Hg lamp. You can 
see green, blue and purple on the right side of the screen.

![](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/020/521/089/f04a8e510abbce2237bbaeb26e1ea8b8_original.png?w=700&fit=max&v=1520824781&auto=format&lossless=true&s=e08ed8213314c4c202bdc1fc98265f77)

_**Calibration : **_

1,  Pre-measurement is occurred to using Hg lamp\* . Software using dummy Pixel No. and wave length. \*Hg-Ar is better.

2\. Result of pre-measurement gives true pixel No. and wave length.

3\. To overwrite a calibration file.  (column A; pixel No., column B wave length)

![](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/020/595/380/f07119b3a2654f31910994b35c0d1582_original.jpg?w=700&fit=max&v=1521428970&auto=format&q=92&s=b853d43362d06cd2e259d319e84e24dc)

![Inner structure](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/020/399/404/9be1e55d57324949e7b8c3d1c1f236e1_original.JPG?w=700&fit=max&v=1519865660&auto=format&q=92&s=3d7eb4a6998eec41bdeea8ac4dd316d6)Inner structure

_**Structure**_ : The internal structure consists of a spectroscope, a CCD sensor, and a  PCB substrate.

**_Contents_** : 

-Spectrometer (slit, filter : customer's choice) x1

-Software (English or Japanese) x1

-Hex wrench x1
