We are getting a jump on the leaves this year, and LEAFFEST is happening NEXT WEEK. We are pleased to offer a full afternoon and evening of free public workshops by Chris Fastie, Ned Horning, Jeff Warren, and Don Blair on **Saturday, September 7**. These will focus on [Infragram photography](http://publiclab.org/wiki/infragram) and quantitative pole aerial photography.  But the event lasts all weekend, with related activities both days, and a [home garden](http://publiclab.org/notes/cfastie/08-11-2013/garden-pap-map) cookout and camping on Saturday night. Everyone is welcome. There will be plenty of opportunity for adding to the agenda, so come prepared to teach and learn.

##Details of the [workshops and logistics are here](http://publiclab.org/wiki/leaffest).

Please register so you can let us know which events you might be attending. Don't worry if your plans are tentative, so are ours. But it helps to have an idea of numbers.
