by [gonzoearth](/profile/gonzoearth) | June 29, 2013 05:00

See how the community is keeping busy in this edition of our newsletter. Summer has officially arrived in the Northern Hemisphere with the Summer Solstice occurring this week. With the outdoors in mind, we have put together several new kit bundles in the Public Lab Store. More on that and other current events below. 
 
**Infragram** 

[Last week to back the Infragram campaign](http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/publiclab/infragram-the-infrared-photography-project?ref=card.) Do you know anyone that might want to purchase an Infragram camera as a gift? Tell a friend, the money raised from the campaign will directly support Public Lab. 

New research from Chris Fastie on anlayzing the channels of infragram aerial imagery for optimal plant health detection. [Infrablue Haze](http://publiclab.org/notes/cfastie/06-26-2013/infrablue-haze). 

A series of notes on disassembling and [converting Gopro cameras](http://publiclab.org/notes/gonzoearth/06-24-2013/converting-gopro-cameras) for multispectral imagery.
