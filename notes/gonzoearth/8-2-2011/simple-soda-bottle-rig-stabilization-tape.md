# simple soda bottle rig stabilization with tape

by [gonzoearth](../../../profile/gonzoearth) | August 02, 2011 05:01

August 02, 2011 05:01 | Tags: [replication:13948](../tag/replication:13948)

----

In an effort to stabilize the camera further, a simple application of tape along the tether and the side of the soda bottle has shown to be a worthwhile modification towards improved DIY aerial acquisition. In this image the camera is already shooting and a piece of tape is applied as a final pre-flight platform preparation.
