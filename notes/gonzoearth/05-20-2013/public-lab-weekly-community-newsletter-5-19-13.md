# Public Lab Weekly Community Newsletter 5.19.13

A busy week in the community including a compelling story of grassroots mapping in Uganda and some amazing spectrometry research.

**Upcoming Events**

[June 2 Science Hack Day NYC: balloon mapping workshop](http://publiclab.org/notes/liz/05-13-2013/science-hack-day-nyc-balloon-mapping-workshop)
A NYC a local event that is part of World Science Hack Day, which coincides with both the World Science Festival and National Day of Civic Hacking.

**Mapping**
Incredible report on the [using balloon mapping by the JuaKali Craft Village in Kampala, Uganda to temporarily stall eviction proceedings.](http://publiclab.org/notes/mlamadrid/05-15-2013/tool-for-stalling-mapping)

Report from [Aerial Mapping workshop at the Triennale Design Museum. Milano, Italia](http://publiclab.org/notes/Costantino/05-15-2013/aerial-mapping-workshop-triennale-milano)

Mapping an Atlantic coastal restoration site at [Folly Beach, South Carolina](http://publiclab.org/notes/adam-griffith/5-14-2013/folly-beach-sc-detailed-look-3-million-beach-restoration)

Why worry about flying your camera when you can simply [stick a really long pole in the air!](http://publiclab.org/notes/mathew/05-17-2013/pole-photography)

**Spectrometry**

Using the Spectroscope for Analysis of Concentration (Beer's Law). Great [writeup on using the PLOTS desktop spectrometer and the spectralworkbench.org to determine the concentration of an unknown sample.](http://publiclab.org/notes/straylight/05-13-2013/using-the-spectroscope-for-analysis-of-concentration-beer-s-law)

[In Search of Spectrometer Measurement and Calibration](http://publiclab.org/notes/stoft/05-14-2013/in-search-of-spectrometer-measurement-and-calibration)

Notes on [adding external attenuation for the spectrometer's incoming light](http://publiclab.org/notes/stoft/05-14-2013/in-search-of-spectrometer-attenuators)

[Laser oil fluorescence proof of concept](http://publiclab.org/notes/warren/05-14-2013/laser-oil-fluorescence-proof-of-concept)

A [preview of the DesktopSpectrometry Kit Version 2.5](http://publiclab.org/notes/warren/05-14-2013/desktop-spectrometry-kit-v2-5-tests)
