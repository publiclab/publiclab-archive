Many different links to share this week from the ongoing infrared photography fundraising and some of the highlights from a busy week of research, news, and events.

**Infragram Campaign**
As the Kickstarter campaign continues we discuss passing 1K backers and a new “Combo Kit” reward level in the [latest project update.](http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/publiclab/infragram-the-infrared-photography-project/posts) [We are tracking Infragram press here.](http://publiclab.org/wiki/infragram-press)

**Atlanta Maps**
We have just published a series of great MapKnitter maps around the city of Atlanta, Georgia. They include maps from the Georgia tech campus, the Oakland cemetery, and a high altitude map of Midtown Atlanta. [Map Archive.](http://publiclab.org/archive)

Narrated instructional [video time-lapse of a MapKnitter project.](http://publiclab.org/notes/gonzoearth/05-29-2013/time-lapse-video-of-a-mapknitter-project)
