# Public Lab Community Newsletter 6.23.2013

by [gonzoearth](../../../profile/gonzoearth) | June 24, 2013 05:33

June 24, 2013 05:33 | Tags: [newsletter](../tag/newsletter), [response:8141](../tag/response:8141)

----

In this edition of the Public Lab community newsletter; a heavy dose of Infragram with a sprinkling of mapping videos. Lots of research news related to the Public Lab infrared photography kickstarter campaign that now [has only 12 days remaining](http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/publiclab/infragram-the-infrared-photography-project?ref=card).  

**Infragram** 

Will Ward shares some incredible [Infragram tidepooling photography](http://publiclab.org/notes/wward1400/06-14-2013/infragram-tidepooling).

Chris Fastie looks carefully at [white balance in infragram images](http://publiclab.org/notes/cfastie/06-17-2013/white-balance).  
Histograms. First a discussion on [tweaking histograms](http://publiclab.org/notes/cfastie/06-13-2013/histograms), next on to [engineering them](http://publiclab.org/notes/cfastie/06-21-2013/engineered-histograms). In addition a [deeper look](http://publiclab.org/notes/cfastie/06-20-2013/ndvi-from-infrablue) at the actual infragram histogram values.

Jeff Warren considers infragram image channels and inexpensive CMOS cameras (http://publiclab.org/notes/warren/06-18-2013/continuing-difficulties-with-infrablue-in-cheaper-cmos-cameras). And [swapping channels](http://publiclab.org/notes/warren/06-21-2013/channel-swapping) as a measure against difficulties with these cameras.
 
converting small cameras for infragram(http://publiclab.org/notes/warren/06-19-2013/small-camera-conversion-tests). 

**Events** 

Jay Holmes of YouthCaN(Youth Communicating and Networking) [reports from the group’s first mapping outing](http://publiclab.org/notes/jholmes5/06-23-2013/first-youtcan-trial-kite-mapping) in Brooklyn.

**Videos** 

Caterina Scaramelli’s documentary on kite mapping and the Cocodrie barnraising is [now online](http://publiclab.org/notes/scaramel/6-13-2013/video-kitemappers).  

Public Lab DIY map making presentation at [TEDxUCDavis from the University of California at Davis](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=22brTRmlmhE).
