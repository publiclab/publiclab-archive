Conocer el estado ambiental de un ambiente acuático que nos interese. Las personas que amamos el medio ambiente nos gusta saber en que estado se encuentra. De esta manera, podremos saber a lo largo del tiempo y el espacio como evolucionan. Así sabremos si es necesario recomponerlo o conservarlo.

#English

## Purpose

With this mobile application we can know the environmental status of an aquatic environment that interests us. People who love the environment like to know what state it is in. With this tool, we will be able to know over time and space how they evolve. This way we will know if it is necessary to carry out activities to rebuild it or for its conservation.
  
## Materials needed

* A mobile phone with Android as the operating system


## Step 1

Download the application from the app store :point_down:  
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ilpla.appear

## Step 2 :pencil:
