# Infrared balloon image reveals Gowanus plume

by [liz](/profile/liz) | August 03, 2011 18:32

August 03, 2011 18:32 | Tags: [gowanus](/tag/gowanus), [nrg](/tag/nrg), [infrared](/tag/infrared), [plume](/tag/plume), [watchdog](/tag/watchdog), [great-photos](/tag/great-photos)

----

Two photos (one visible, one near-infrared) taken by balloon aerial rig on Sunday July 31 were composited to reveal this plume.

Original visible image in MapMill: 
http://mapmill.org/sites/2011-7-31-brooklyn-gowanus-vis-b/IMG_2090.JPG
Original near-infrared image in MapMill: 
http://mapmill.org/sites/2011-7-31-brooklyn-gowanus-ir/IMG_2039.JPG

<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/lizbarry/6005645227/" title="original photo of Gowanus by lizbarry, on Flickr"><img src="http://farm7.static.flickr.com/6150/6005645227_9eeb6cc55c_m.jpg" width="240" height="189" alt="original photo of Gowanus"></a><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/lizbarry/6006276192/" title="near infrared image showing plume by lizbarry, on Flickr"><img src="http://farm7.static.flickr.com/6013/6006276192_f7e9d57197_m.jpg" width="240" height="189" alt="near infrared image showing plume"></a>

The video tutorial on how to composite images like this is linked from the <a href="http://publiclab.org/tool/near-infrared-camera">Near-Infrared Camera Tool page</a> - scroll down to find it. 

These images were collected by the GLAM crew in partnership with the Gowanus Canal Conservancy, and with canoes contributed by the Gowanus Dredgers Canoe Club.
