# Come try spectrometry at Science Hack Day

by [liz](../profile/liz) | May 23, 2014 21:41

May 23, 2014 21:41 | Tags: [new-york-city](../tag/new-york-city), [spectrometry](../tag/spectrometry), [event](../tag/event)

----

###Event details

Hands on exploration of the principles of spectrometry, a perfect time to try it out for yourself!

* Saturday, May 31, 2-4pm
* location: MAGNET, NYU School of Engineering, 2 MetroTech Center, downtown Brooklyn, NY 11201: [directions](http://www.worldsciencefestival.com/venues/magnet_nyu_school_of_engineering/)
* part of [Science Hack Day: Science In The City](http://www.worldsciencefestival.com/programs/hack-day-science-in-the-city/)
* This will be part of the Citizen Science Explorers Program, where you can learn how to participate in a range of urban science projects
* the day-long event will be running from 10:00 AM - midnight, but **our spectrometry workshop will be 2-4pm.** 
* foldable and desktop spectrometers will be available to purchase if you'd like to assemble your own!

###Background

This workshop will be held as part of World Science Festival:
