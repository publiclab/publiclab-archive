# EDGI FOIA event at CUNY in New York City & online

by [liz](/profile/liz) | January 20, 2017 22:30

January 20, 2017 22:30 | Tags: [new-york-city](/tag/new-york-city), [event](/tag/event), [live-call](/tag/live-call), [cuny](/tag/cuny), [edgi](/tag/edgi)

This event will be at the [Center for Urban Environmental Reform][1], hosted by [CUNY School of Law][2] as part of the [Environmental Data and Governance Initiative][3] with Public Lab.

Note: For visitors to CUNY: bring your photo ID

Note: We will not be archiving a list of attendees, although feel free to RSVP here if you wish

\*\*\*\*

- CUNY School of Law

- Long Island City, NY 11101

**SCHEDULE:**

40 minutes: [Lauren Kurtz][6], Executive Director of the Climate Science Defense Fund (the people who represented scientists in the wake of Climate Gate) will present "FOIA: Know Your Rights and Responsibilities."

20 minute Q&A including from online participants

--5 minute break--

40 minutes: Sarah Lamdan, of CUNY School of Law and the Environmental Data and Governance Initiative, will present "How to FOIA environmental records." This presentation will include a printed handout, with online version to follow. She has a book coming out on environmental data access soon!

20 minutes Q&A including from online participants![image description][7]
