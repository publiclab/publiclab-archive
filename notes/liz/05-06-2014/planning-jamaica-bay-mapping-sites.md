**This note is posted on behalf of [Eymund](/profile/eymund).**

Eymund is working with Gena Wirth (Public Lab organizer) and Becky Boger of Brooklyn College to plan out some summertime mapping excursions on Jamaica Bay. This series is planned as **an exploration of historic wetlands that now exist as "dredged wetlands" and "filled water".** The question driving this investigation is about where wetland restoration will be most possible / effective / valuable.
