Are you an educator interested in using Public Lab resources in your classroom? Join us for a special Public Lab 101 where we'll show you how to:navigate the Public Lab website

- share your/student work
- connect with the Public Lab community from your classroom
- use Public Lab resources to enhance creativity and project design for students

This virtual workshop is being offered twice to best suit your availability. We'll host **session 1 on Thursday, May 20 at 5 pm EDT / 2 pm PDT** ([find your timezone here!](https://everytimezone.com/s/4d46ea37)) and **session 2 on Tuesday, May 25 at 6 pm PDT / 8 pm CDT** ([find your timezone here!](https://everytimezone.com/s/fd65d846)).

[To register and learn more about the workshop, please sign up using this form](https://forms.gle/mPuGAnhoP9RtxXG97 "https://forms.gle/mPuGAnhoP9RtxXG97").
