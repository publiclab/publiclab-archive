**Public Lab Community Newsletter 11.03.13**

A couple big announcements in the newsletter this week--including Public Lab an update on our 501(c)3 status, and an Organizer’s trip to Rwanda! Hope everyone had a great week and a fun, safe Halloween!

**Announcements**

We are happy to announce that after a 22 month wait, Public Lab has been approved as a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization by the IRS!

Public Lab is proud to be selected as an [Honorable Mention](http://challenge.bfi.org/2013Finalist_PublicLab) amongst a great group of Finalists for the Buckminster Fuller Challenge! More info and a summary of the great review team comments we received [here](http://publiclab.org/notes/Becki/11-03-2013/public-lab-named-honorable-mention-in-2013-buckminster-fuller-challenge). The award ceremony will happen at Cooper Union in New York City on November 18, 2013 at 7:00 pm. RSVP for the event at if you’re in the area and wish to attend: [http://bfi.org/2013-ceremony](http://bfi.org/2013-ceremony).

[A really nice Public Lab shout out](http://www.raspberrypi.org/archives/5146) from our friends at Raspberry Pi, related to the Pi NoIR. 

[Shannon and Becki compiled evaluations](http://publiclab.org/notes/Shannon/10-27-2013/2013-barnraising-evaluation-summary) from the 2013 Barnraising. Thanks everyone for your feedback--we look forward to using it to make the next event even better!

**Upcoming events**

Organizer Don Blair will be representing Infragram at [ICT4Ag](http://ict4ag.org/en/) in Rwanda, Nov 4-8.
