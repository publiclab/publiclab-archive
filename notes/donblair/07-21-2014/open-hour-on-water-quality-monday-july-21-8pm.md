## Open Hour call

Hey Folks!

### When

Tonight, July 21st, at 8 PM

It's a Google Hangout -- so, everywhere!

We'll be talking about **water quality issues** on the Hangout -- things we've been up to, but also generally whatever anyone on the call would like to chat about. You can join the Hangout here:

http://publiclab.org/wiki/openhour

[Parts and Crafts](http://partsandcrafts.org/about-us/our-space/)
