# Riffleito-TH

(Pronounced like you'd pronounce plural of "Riffleito", if your mouth were full of crackers.)

The "TH" stands for "Temp and Humidity".

A Riffle connected to a DHT22 sensor, logging data w/ timestamps on a battery.

The on-board RTC has a built-in temp sensor; these graphs compare the RTC temp sensor with the DHT22 sensor.
