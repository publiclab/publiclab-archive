###What I want to do:
I will be presenting material published on the Public Lab site to the good people of Atlanta.  
###My attempt and results
This will be a poster presentation showing how we have used the WheeStat potentiostat to measure lead in water and airborne hydrogen sulfide and hopefully getting the word out there about what we do.
###When is this wonderful Maker event?
Saturday, October 4, from 10 am till 5 pm and Sunday, October 5 from noon till  5.
###Where will it all happen?
125 W Trinity Place, Decatur, GA 30030.
