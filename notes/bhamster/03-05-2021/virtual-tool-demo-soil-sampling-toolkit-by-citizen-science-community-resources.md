## Event details

**When**: Wednesday, March 31, 10:00am PT / 12:00pm CT / 1:00pm ET / 5:00pm UTC / [other time zones](https://everytimezone.com/s/44470e74). This event will run for approximately 45 minutes.

**Where**: <a class="btn btn-primary btn-lg" href="https://us02web.zoom.us/j/82194125176?pwd=Zmc0emZsMWlXdUNZOHJiY3ZUZi9Odz09">Click here to Join Zoom Meeting</a>

**What**: Public Lab is thrilled to host a **virtual tool demo featuring [Jackie James](https://publiclab.org/profile/jjcreedon), founder of [Citizen Science Community Resources](https://csresources.org/), and their Soil Sampling ToolKit**! Just in time for spring for those of us north of the Equator. Come and learn about how the soil sampling toolkit works, how it came to be, and how it's been used by communities to address local concerns about soil. There will be a Q&A at the end of the demo.
