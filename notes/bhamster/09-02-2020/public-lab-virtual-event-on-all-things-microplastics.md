Save-the-date for a virtual event on microplastics as we wrap up a [microplastics review](https://publiclab.org/notes/bhamster/08-19-2020/reviewing-synthesizing-microplastics-research-on-public-lab)!

#### Who is this event for?

If you're interested in microplastics and want to know more about the latest in community monitoring methods, tool development, and advocacy, please join us! Whether you're new to the topic of microplastics or you're a seasoned researcher, this event is open to everyone and an opportunity to **connect with each other**.

#### Event details

When: September 23, 8am PT / 10am CT / 11am ET / 3pm UTC / [other time zones](https://everytimezone.com/s/1f436f9c)

Where: <a class="btn btn-primary btn-lg" href="https://us02web.zoom.us/j/82506627571?pwd=bTB4VmxtR3FtalZtRGk5OC9IOWZCQT09">Click here to Join Zoom Meeting</a>

What: a time to welcome each other to the topic and share stories, highlights in research and advocacy, and ideas for what to do next.
