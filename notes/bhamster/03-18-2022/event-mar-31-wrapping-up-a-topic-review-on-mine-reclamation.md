Join us to wrap up our latest [research area review on mine reclamation](https://publiclab.org/notes/bhamster/01-31-2022/launching-a-research-area-review-on-mine-reclamation). We've been addressing this topic for the last couple months by asking questions, posting research notes, and talking about local projects on Open Calls.

For this event, we'll give a brief presentation highlighting conversations and resources on mine reclamation at Public Lab and beyond. A facilitated community discussion will follow. **Anyone interested in mining and mine reclamation and remediation is encouraged to attend!** People who are looking for resources and a supportive community might find this call especially helpful.

**When**: **Thursday, March 31, 10am PT / 12pm CT / 1pm ET / 5pm UTC / [other time zones](https://everytimezone.com/s/477919d3)**. This event will run for approximately 1.5 hours.

**Where:** <a class="btn btn-primary btn-lg" href="https://zoom.us/j/99311328910?pwd=RWFncHdtdjFvSkxIVEN6Mm1vWGJxZz09">Click here to join Zoom Meeting</a>

**What:**

- 0 - 15 mins: Welcome and introductions
- 15 - 45 mins: We'll present resources on what mine reclamation is and approaches for community science monitoring
- 45 - 90 mins: Open and facilitated community discussion time with @laurel_mire. Anyone is welcome to share questions, tips and stories, and meet others who are interested in mine reclamation
