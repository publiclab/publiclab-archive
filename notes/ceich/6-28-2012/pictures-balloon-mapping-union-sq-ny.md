# Pictures of Balloon Mapping Union Sq NY

by [C_Eich](../../../profile/C_Eich) | June 28, 2012 14:19

June 28, 2012 14:19 | Tags: [balloon-mapping](../tag/balloon-mapping), [diy-balloon](../tag/diy-balloon), [camera-rig](../tag/camera-rig)

These are documentary photos I shot of the live-view Balloon Mapping on May Day 2012 in Union Square in New York City.

There were many thousands of people around and lots of people were enlisted on the spot to help rig up the balloons.
