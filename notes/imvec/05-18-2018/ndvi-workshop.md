## What we want to do

Introduce the assistants to the main concepts around NDVI and plant health monitoring, camera hacking around it and image processing using Infragram.

## When,where and how

**March 24th - 19:00** Spanish time
The workshop will take place in **three places**.

- Barcelona: HangarVisual Arts Research and Production Center, Emilia Coranty 16\.
- Donosti: Tabakalera Contemporary Culture Center, Plaza de las cigarreras.
- Online: contact the team at imvec(at)[tutanota.com](http://tutanota.com) ifyou want to conect online.

Session will be held in spanish.
