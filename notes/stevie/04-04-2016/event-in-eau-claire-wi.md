# Event in Eau Claire, WI

by [stevie](../profile/stevie) April 04, 2016 20:23

April 04, 2016 20:23 | Tags: [event](../tag/event), [wisconsin](../tag/wisconsin), [air-quality](../tag/air-quality), [event:rsvp](../tag/event:rsvp), [mid-west](../tag/mid-west), [rsvp:marlokeno](../tag/rsvp:marlokeno), [date:04-23-2016](../tag/date:04-23-2016)

Interested in exploring water monitoring techniques that can help us report frac sand water pollution events? Want to learn about low cost dust monitoring and technique development? Join us Saturday, **April 23rd at the Eau Claire Public Library from 10:30am-12:30pm** for an event exploring these projects! 

From **1:30-3:00pm** we'll also be running a water sensing activity exploring turbidity and reporting runoff events in streams. Join us for one or both parts of the event!

###About the event
Following up on the [Frac Sand Tools for Action](https://publiclab.org/notes/stevie/07-20-2015/frac-sand-tools-for-action-event) event and the work that is being done on [low cost dust monitoring](https://publiclab.org/wiki/passive-pm) and water monitoring, Public Lab is hosting another event! This event is open to people of all ages and interests, so whether you're a concerned citizen interested in reporting water pollution or a hobbyist interested [trap cams and electronics](https://publiclab.org/notes/donblair/03-23-2016/ttl-serial-jpeg-cameras-microcontrollers), this is the event for you. 

**Where:** The Chippewa Room in the L.E. Phillips Memorial Public Library in Eau Claire, WI.

**When:** Saturday April 23rd </br>
_10:30am-12:30pm_ in the Library exploring monitoring techniques.</br>
_12:30_ Lunch @ The Galloway Grill<br>
_1:30pm-3:00pm_ Exploratory field deployment of Turbidity tool! We'll be between the river and the library. Looking for us? Give a ring: 603.273.7027
