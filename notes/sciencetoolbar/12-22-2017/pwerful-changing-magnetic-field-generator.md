Powerful changing magnetic field generator.

A simple way to create a powerfull changing magnetic field using a few materials.

Materials used to create changing magnetic field generator : neodymium magnet sphere 26 mm, 5 V dc motor, usb cable, 3,7 V battery, screw pot magnet case (from a neodymium screw pot magnet), plastic connector.
