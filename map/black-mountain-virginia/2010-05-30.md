}</style><h3>Notes</h3><p>Jim Wiley from <a href="http://wileyslastresort.com">wileyslastresort.com</a> guided our mapping party out to the site in his 4x4 Jeep Wrangler. The purpose of the balloon mapping trial was to try balloon aerial mapping at a mountain top removal (MTR) reclamation site. 

First, we had to create an improvised tether while in the field in order to fly high altitudes. In the somewhat rural area of Eastern Kentucky that we were in we had access to a farm supply store.
